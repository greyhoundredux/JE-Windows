WELCOME TO JAMES ENGLISH WINDOW CLEANING & PROPERTY MAINTENANCE

James English Window Cleaning & Property Maintenance was established, and has been operating across the North Cotswolds since 2006.

Through providing a reliable, friendly and very professional service, the business has grown a large client base spanning a wide area.

We cover towns within Gloucestershire, Warwickshire, Worcestershire and Oxfordshire.

OUR SERVICES

Window Cleaning

We currently clean windows for over 200 satisfied clients across a wide area. These jobs range from small bungalows to large hotels, and the frequency of clean is completely up to the customer. They can range from one-off after building work, to every couple of weeks as regular maintenance.

Whilst window cleaning is the bread and butter of our business, we are also happy to offer:

Conservatory Windows and Roof Cleaning

Emptying and Cleaning of Gutters

Facia Board Cleaning
